# HLYST Audio Cartridge — Industrial Design Specification

## Overview

A premium collectible album cartridge designed for a portable audio player. The cartridge houses a standard microSD card inside a custom shell, turning digital music into a tangible, collectible physical format.

## Dimensions & Form Factor

| Property       | Value                |
|----------------|----------------------|
| Width          | 28 mm                |
| Height         | 28 mm                |
| Thickness      | ~2 mm                |
| Silhouette     | Rounded square       |
| Top corners    | Softly rounded       |
| Bottom edge    | Flat                 |

## Materials & Finish

- **Frame:** Matte dark graphite plastic
- **Chamfers:** Clean, precise — communicates high-end industrial design
- **Surface feel:** Premium, not toy-like
- **Branding:** "HLYST" wordmark centered along bottom edge of front frame, lightly embossed into plastic, clean modern sans-serif font

## Front Face — Album Artwork Panel

- Inset square panel covering nearly the entire front face
- Artwork sits flush or very slightly recessed within a subtle surrounding frame
- Art style reference: colorful, cartoon-surreal aesthetic inspired by Kanye West's *Graduation* cover art
  - Vibrant purples, pinks, oranges, yellows
  - Abstract shapes and characters
  - No readable text or logos from the original — original compositions only

## Connector

- Bottom edge includes a thin connector "tongue" integrated into the shell
- Resembles a microSD-shaped edge contact
- Minimal and understated — should not dominate the design
- Enables insertion into a compatible HLYST player device

## Internal Component

- Standard microSD card housed internally
- Not visible from the outside; the collectible value is the shell and artwork

## Product Photography Direction

### Scene

- Cartridge standing upright on a neutral grey concrete or matte tabletop surface
- Shallow depth of field: cartridge in sharp focus, background in smooth bokeh

### Lighting

- Soft natural or studio lighting
- Gentle edge highlights to emphasize thinness and chamfer geometry
- Subtle reflections on plastic surfaces to communicate premium quality

- Minimalist, high-end product photography
- Campaign-shot aesthetic for a design-forward audio brand
